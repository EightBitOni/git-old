# Pancakes


## Dry Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 3 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Wet Ingredients
- 1 1/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 3 table spoons butter "melted"
- little bit of vanilla extract
