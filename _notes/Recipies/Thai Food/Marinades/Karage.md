# Karage "Japanese Fried Chicken"

![[Pasted image 20220712023225.png]]

- Chicken Thighs
- 3 tbsp sake
- 2 Tbsp mirin
- 3 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp ginger juice
- Mix with katakuriko starch or corn starch and leave over night

Optional: add sesame seeds to marinade 

Deep Fry and serve with slice of lemon
